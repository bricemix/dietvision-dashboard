5 Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. It may be shared only with the following sub-processors, strictly within the scope of service delivery:

🤖 OpenRouter / Google Gemini (AI)

OpenRouter is the API gateway used to route nutritional analysis requests (meal photos) and coaching to third-party AI models, including Google Gemini. OpenRouter and model providers are contractually committed to not using this data for training without explicit consent.

6 Security

  • Encrypted communications via HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Time-limited authentication tokens
  • Data access restricted to authorised personnel
  • Servers hosted in Europe (European Union)
  • Regular encrypted backups
In the event of a data breach likely to affect your rights and freedoms, we commit to notifying you within 72 hours in accordance with GDPR Article 33.

7 Your Rights

📋 Right of access (Art. 15)

Obtain a copy of all your personal data we hold.

✏️ Right to rectification (Art. 16)

Correct inaccurate or incomplete data directly in the app or by contacting us.

🗑️ Right to erasure (Art. 17)

Delete your account and all your data. You can initiate deletion from the app. Complete purge within 30 days.

⏸️ Right to restriction (Art. 18)

Request restriction of processing of your data in certain circumstances.

📦 Right to data portability (Art. 20)

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON).

🚫 Right to object (Art. 21)

Object to certain processing based on our legitimate interest.

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@diet-vision.com. We will respond within one month.

If you believe that the processing of your data does not comply with GDPR, you may lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority (e.g. ICO in the UK, CNIL in France).

8 Cookies

The DietVision mobile app does not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers for targeting purposes.

The website diet-vision.com may use strictly necessary technical cookies for site functionality. No tracking or analytics cookies are placed without your explicit consent.

9 Minors

DietVision is intended for persons aged 15 and over. Users under 15 must obtain parental or legal guardian consent before creating an account. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, contact us immediately at support@diet-vision.com.
